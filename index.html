About Bridge4Meal

We bridge two problems with one platform: unemployment and hunger. Our marketplace connects households and businesses with verified workers while our Meal-Aid program channels a portion of revenue to fund daily nutritious meals for those who need them most.

  • Verified worker profiles & fair jobs
  • Real-time matching & multilingual UI
  • Meal-Aid sponsorships integrated into every transaction

FAQ

How do you ensure food safety?

We follow FSSAI guidelines, temperature controls, and limit holding time. Donors accept a safety checklist before pickup.

Is there a fee?

No fee for donors. Workers may opt for a low subscription that funds verification and operations; every transaction contributes to Meal-Aid.

Where do you operate?

Starting in Meerut with phased expansion to nearby cities based on partner demand and volunteer density.
